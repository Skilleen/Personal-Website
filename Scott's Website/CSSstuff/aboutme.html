Who I Am

Instead of describing myself with fancy adjectives and finding overzealous titles to describe what I do, I figured I would actually just tell you about the guy you may be working with. My name is Scott, I am a second year student at Queen's university in computer science. I was born and raised in Ottawa, but have always had interest of moving elsewhere. Since the age of 15, I have been programming and working with computers. I thrive most in a team environment, I believe it is best being able to split a problem into multiple tasks that each team member can tackle. I have a math driven mind, problem solving is my strong suit. I have no specific desired companies I would like to work to, be it a start up or corporation, my main concern is to be making an impact in the digital world that we live in. I am simple and laid back, I hope to work in a great environment where I can learn and grow through my coworkers. I would love if you would consider me for your company.
